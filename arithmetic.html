Algorithm ArithmeticCoding():


Input: A string.


Output: The arithmetic coding of the string.



calculate all symbols frequency and cumulative density (CDF);


Initialization: low = 0.0, high = 1.0;


while (not EOF) {


n = ReadSymbol();


range = high - low;


high = low + range * CDF(n);


low = low + range * CDF(n-1);


}


output low;
